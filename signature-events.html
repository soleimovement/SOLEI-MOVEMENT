Frequently Asked Questions

Are SOLEI Signature Events open to everyone?

Yes. Unless otherwise stated, SOLEI events are designed to be welcoming and accessible to a range of movement experience levels.

Do I need Pilates experience?

Not at all. Modifications and guidance are provided so both beginners and experienced participants can feel supported.

Do I need to bring a mat?

Mat requirements vary by event. Each event listing will clearly explain whether guests should bring a mat or whether mats are available.

Where are events held?

SOLEI events take place at thoughtfully selected venues throughout South Florida, with future destination experiences possible.

How do I register?

Registration details are included with each event announcement. Most events require advance registration and may have limited capacity.

What happens if an event sells out?

When available, you may join the waitlist or sign up for the SOLEI email list to receive updates about future experiences.

Can I bring a friend?

Absolutely. Each guest should complete their own registration unless the event listing says otherwise.

Are tickets refundable or transferable?

Refund and transfer policies may vary by event and will be clearly stated during registration.
