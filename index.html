Meet Sophia

Hi, I'm Sophia. Movement has changed my life and now it's my passion to help others experience that same transformation.

After years of teaching thousands of classes and working with people from all walks of life, I realized something — people weren't just looking for a workout. They wanted confidence. They wanted connection. They wanted a space where they felt welcomed.

That's exactly why I created SOLEI. My goal is always the same: to help you leave feeling stronger than when you arrived.
