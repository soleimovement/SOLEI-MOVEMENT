Frequently Asked Questions

What types of private events do you offer?

SOLEI creates private Pilates and wellness experiences for birthdays, bachelorettes, bridal weekends, corporate groups, hotels, retreats, apartment communities, brand activations, and other special gatherings.

Where can the event take place?

Events may be held at a private residence, vacation rental, hotel, resort, workplace, event venue, outdoor space, or another approved location. The client is responsible for securing permission to use the space.

How many guests can participate?

Group capacity depends on the location, available space, event format, and equipment needs. Include your estimated guest count in the inquiry form so SOLEI can recommend the best option.

Do guests need Pilates experience?

No. Private events are designed to be welcoming and accessible for mixed experience levels, with modifications provided throughout the class.

Are mats included?

Mats are not automatically included. Guests may bring their own, or mat coordination options may be discussed during planning based on availability and location.

What should guests bring?

Guests should wear comfortable clothing, bring water, and follow any mat or equipment instructions provided before the event.

How far in advance should I inquire?

Inquiries are encouraged as early as possible, especially for weekends, holidays, and destination events. Availability is not guaranteed until the event is officially reserved.

How is the date reserved?

After availability and event details are confirmed, the date is reserved once the agreement is completed and the required deposit is received.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation, rescheduling, and weather policies will be included in the event agreement before payment is submitted.

Can SOLEI travel?

Yes. Travel may be available throughout South Florida and for select destination events. Additional travel fees may apply.
