More than a workout.

Private Pilates creates space to slow down, reconnect with your body, and build confidence through intentional movement.

Whether you are working toward a specific goal or simply creating time for yourself, every session is designed to help you leave feeling stronger than when you arrived.

Personalized. Intentional. Built for You.

Every session is shaped around your experience level, goals, movement history, and how you want to feel. There is no one-size-fits-all routine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do sessions take place?

Private sessions are available at an agreed-upon location in South Florida, including your home, a private studio, hotel, or vacation rental. Travel availability and any additional fees will be confirmed before booking.

What should I bring?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move freely and bring water. Mat and equipment details will be confirmed before your session.

Do I need Pilates experience?

Not at all. Sessions are personalized for all experience levels, including complete beginners.

How long is each session?

Sessions are approximately 50 to 60 minutes, depending on the service booked.

Can I book with a friend?

Yes. Partner sessions and small private-group sessions are available.
